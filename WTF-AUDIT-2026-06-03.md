## TIER 0 — THE ONE REAL SECURITY BUG (fix first)

### ✅ `/api/genome` PATCH + `/api/genome/event` POST — unauthenticated cross-user write
`server.js:9446` (PATCH) and `~9472` (POST). No auth middleware. Takes
`userId` straight from `req.body` and does `INSERT … ON CONFLICT(user_id) DO UPDATE`
on `user_genome_state`. **Any unauthenticated caller can overwrite any user's
genome state** by supplying a `userId`. Read directly — this is real.
**Fix:** wrap both in `requireAuth`, derive `userId` from the session, not the body.

### ⚠️ Other unguarded state-mutating endpoints (lens-reported, not re-read)
- `/api/lineage/reload` (server.js:11508) — POST reloads lineage from disk, no auth.
- `/api/soul/reload` (server.js:7428) — POST reloads soul, no auth (read-only-ish, low sev).
These are lower impact (no per-user data write) but still unauthenticated side-effects.
